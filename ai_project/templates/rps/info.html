How do you play?

Rock, paper, scissors is a legendary hand game that many of us played with friends in childhood.

Rules are simple. You choose one of three hand gesture shapes (✊, ✋, ✌), which you form in the camera frame. Your goal is to guess a shape that beats your opponent's choice:

Rock beats Scissors   (✊ → ✌)
Paper covers Rock   (✋ → ✊)
Scissors cuts Paper    (✌ → ✋)
